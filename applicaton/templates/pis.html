MEaSURE Deer and Tick Survey

Welcome to the tick and deer survey

This survey intends to gain information of ticks and deer across the UK. We would like you to share your knowledge of ticks and deer in {{town}}. Here we will ask you questions on ticks and deer in the local area.

We do not ask personal or traceable questions. Although we do ask how people generally spend their time here, this is so we can place your tick and deer answers in the context of how you use this area. This will take less than 3 mins to complete.
